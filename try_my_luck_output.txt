### Cheesy Egg and Bread Casserole

This Cheesy Egg and Bread Casserole is a comforting dish, perfect for a filling dinner. It pairs well with a fresh salad or roasted vegetables. This recipe will make 8 servings, and any necessary additional ingredients have been included in the shopping cart.

#### Ingredients:
- 8 pieces of bread
- 200 grams cheese, grated (reserve some for topping)
- 8 eggs
- 200 grams salt 
- 200 ml milk
- 1 teaspoon black pepper (adjust to taste)
- 2 tablespoons oil (for greasing the baking dish)
  
#### Preparation Instructions:
1. **Preheat the Oven**: Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
   
2. **Prepare the Bread**: Cut the bread into cubes or small pieces and set aside. Grease a large baking dish with oil.

3. **Make the Egg Mixture**: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt (to taste, avoid using the entire 200 grams), and black pepper until well combined.

4. **Assemble the Casserole**:
   - Layer half of the bread pieces at the bottom of the greased baking dish.
   - Sprinkle a generous portion of the grated cheese over the bread.
   - Pour half of the egg mixture evenly over the cheese and bread.
   - Repeat the layering with the remaining bread, cheese, and egg mixture, making sure the top layer is well-covered with cheese.

5. **Bake**: Place the baking dish in the preheated oven. Bake for 30-40 minutes, or until the egg is fully set and the top is golden brown and bubbly.

6. **Serve**: Remove from the oven and allow to cool slightly before serving. Cut into portions and serve warm.

**Optional Tips/Variations**:
- Add diced vegetables like bell peppers, onions, or tomatoes for extra flavor and nutrition.
- For a richer flavor, use different types of cheese such as mozzarella or cheddar.
- You can also incorporate cooked meats like bacon or sausage for a heartier dish.

#### Shopping Cart:

This straightforward casserole combines accessibility and flavor by utilizing the available ingredients efficiently. Enjoy your hearty and comforting dinner!
