### Cheese and Chicken Bread Omelette

#### Ingredients:
- 50 grams cheese
- 1 egg
- 200 grams salt (a pinch needed)
- 400 grams chicken
- 4 pieces bread
- Cooking oil (for frying)

#### Instructions:

1. **Prepare the Chicken:**
   - Slice the 400 grams of chicken into small, bite-sized pieces.
   - Season the chicken pieces with a pinch of salt.
   - In a frying pan, heat a small amount of oil over medium heat.
     - Add the chicken pieces to the pan.
     - Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and no longer pink in the middle.
   - Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.

2. **Prepare the Cheese Omelette:**
   - Crack the egg into a bowl and beat it until the yolk and white are fully combined.
   - Add a pinch of salt to the egg and mix well.
   - Grate the 50 grams of cheese and mix it into the egg mixture.
   - In the same frying pan, add a little more oil if necessary and pour in the egg mixture.
   - Allow it to cook for 1-2 minutes until the edges start to set.
   - Add the cooked chicken pieces on top of the omelette and fold it over to encase the chicken.
   - Cook for an additional 2 minutes until the omelette is fully cooked and the cheese has melted.

3. **Prepare the Bread:**
   - Toast the 4 pieces of bread in a toaster or on a frying pan with a little oil until they are golden brown.

4. **Assembling the Dish:**
   - Cut the omelette into three even portions.
   - Serve each portion on a plate with one toasted bread piece on the side.
   - If desired, place an additional piece of bread on top of each omelette to create a sandwich.

5. **Serve and Enjoy:**
   - Serve the Cheese and Chicken Bread Omelette warm as a satisfying lunch.
