**Recipe Title:** Cheesy Chicken Bread Bake

**Ingredients:**
- 300 grams Cheese
- 400 grams Chicken
- 5 pieces Bread
- 4 dl Milk
- 4 Eggs
- Salt, to taste
- Pepper, to taste
- 2 tablespoons Oil

**Instructions:**

1. **Prepare the Chicken**
    - Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a pan over medium heat.
    - Season the 400 grams of chicken with salt and pepper.
    - Cook the chicken until it is golden brown and fully cooked, then set aside to cool. Once cooled, shred the chicken into small pieces.

2. **Prepare the Bread Base**
    - Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
    - Cut the 5 pieces of bread into cubes.
    - Lightly toast the bread cubes by spreading them on a baking sheet and placing them in the preheated oven for about 5-7 minutes, or until they are slightly crispy.

3. **Make the Egg and Milk Mixture**
    - In a large bowl, beat the 4 eggs.
    - Add 4 dl of milk to the bowl with the eggs, stirring to combine.
    - Season the mixture with a pinch of salt and pepper.

4. **Assemble the Bake**
    - Grease a medium-sized baking dish with 1 tablespoon of oil.
    - Spread the toasted bread cubes evenly in the bottom of the dish.
    - Layer the shredded chicken on top of the bread cubes.
    - Pour the egg and milk mixture over the bread and chicken layers, ensuring everything is evenly covered.
    - Sprinkle 300 grams of cheese over the top.

5. **Bake the Dish**
    - Place the assembled dish in the preheated oven.
    - Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the cheese is melted and golden brown on top.
    - Let the dish rest for a few minutes before serving.

6. **Serve**
    - Cut the Cheesy Chicken Bread Bake into 4 equal servings.
    - Plate each serving and enjoy your dinner!
